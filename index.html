Who We Are

Four Decades of
Packaging Excellence

Established in 1980, Bharat Plastics & Button Works (Nirav Enterprises) is one of Maharashtra's most trusted names in plastic packaging manufacturing.


Under the guidance of founder Mr. Bharat Shah and Director Mr. Nirav Shah, we have grown into a full-scale manufacturer serving government bodies, construction giants and global logistics operators. Our Bhiwandi plant runs state-of-the-art extrusion lines, weaving looms, stitching and printing machinery - producing a defect-free range at industrial scale.

We offer complete customisation - size, colour, design, cut and base material - so every client gets exactly what they need. Quality is verified through tensile testing, electronic weighing and denier testing before any product ships.
